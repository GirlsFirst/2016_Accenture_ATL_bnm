" Where Modern and Classic Collide "
    Gabí Lea is a fashion powerhouse, offering timeless collections that encompass both Men's and Women's fashion. In addition to high fashion retail of "ready to wear" apparel and accessories, Couture options are available upon request, designed and constructed in-house. Additional options include, but are not limited to, evening wear, formal wear, casual wear, bridal wear, and more. Gabí Lea skillfully combines modern, but classic style and is the go to destination for the fashion forward style leaders who have an appreciation for timeless as well as avant-garde. Gabí Lea is a brand with visionary, sophisticated direction that is committed to continuing to elevate and innovate the "Fashion Experience."
